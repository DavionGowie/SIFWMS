<p>

Smart intelligent food waste management software, is a smart system for managing food waste that was 
made to help solve one of the world's most pressing sustainability
problems: food waste.  More than 1.3 billion tonnes of food are wasted 
every year around the world. This hurts the environment, costs money, 
and makes food insecurity worse.

Smart inelligent food waste management software uses new technologies like the Internet of Things (IoT), 
artificial intelligence (AI), cloud computing, and data analytics 
to keep an eye on food waste, cut it down, and find new uses for it.  
It gives homes, businesses, and cities the tools they need to keep 
track of waste, learn more, and make better use of resources. 
and redistribute surplus food to those in need.</p>
